Can I return an item?

Yes! We accept returns for any reason within 30 days of your order. Just send us email with your order number and we'll send you a return label.

In how much time can I get my refund?

Once we receive confirmation that you returned the product we will initiate refund process and you will get your refund in 2 to 3 days.

When will I receive my book?

You can expect your item to be delivered within 5 to 7 business days. We use UPS priority shipping and pack your order by hand to make sure that everything is correct. If you need something faster let us know!

Is it possible to change my delivery address once the order is placed?

Yes, you can update your delivery address after placing the order. You have to update before the product is being delivered.

What is shipping cost?

It depends on the number of items that has to be delivered. For 1 item we dont charge any shipping price and for 10 books we charge 10 dollars.
