Who we are:

Reader's Paradise is one of the fastest growing and largest book selling websites in the city. It was launched in April, 2021 with an aim to promote the book reading culture by offering a wide variety of books from latest releases to old ones, from facts to fiction and for young to old generations. Our motto is to help books reach to every household room, for 'A room without book is like body without soul."

Are you an avid reader? Do you have a passion for books or, are you new to the reading world? Choose from a wide ranging variety of books. Find the latest editions and top sellers. Only by giving are you able to receive more than you already have. In future, we are aiming to offer you a book gifting feature for where you can return your pre-owned books, get your refund and help others to get refurbished books with lucrative discounts.
